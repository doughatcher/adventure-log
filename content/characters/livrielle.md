Livrielle Enafrya moves through the world with the particular bearing of someone who has learned that elegance and survival are not mutually exclusive. An Eladrin fighter trained in the Samurai tradition — a discipline about presence as much as technique, about the unsheathed quality of attention a warrior brings to everything, not just combat.

She watched the arrival at Shard C with the particular stillness of someone committing a thing to memory. The Sea of Governance is the kind of place that rewards that kind of attention — where the architecture of power is written into the water itself, and those who do not read it carefully tend to drown in ways they did not anticipate. Livrielle reads carefully. That much is evident.

Among the party she is the one you notice not because she demands it, but because she cannot help it. In the small domestic shuffle of a session's end — gear equipped, attunements settled, the party making itself ready for what comes next — there was something almost tender about watching her prepare. Whatever she is preparing for, she means to meet it properly dressed.
