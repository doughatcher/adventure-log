Granite is a name that suits someone who does not move unless they mean to. Little is known yet about this figure beyond their presence in the company of an unlikely group pushing toward a corrupted and increasingly dangerous world. They arrived at the Sea of Governance alongside the others, and that is, for now, enough of a foundation to build on.
