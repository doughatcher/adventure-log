Edwin arrived at the Shard C with the group, and there is something about him that suggests arrival is a thing he has done many times — in many senses of the word. He is the kind of person whose history you piece together from small details: a comment here, a hesitation there. The Sea of Governance is a place where histories have weight. Edwin's, whatever it is, will matter here.
