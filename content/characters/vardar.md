Vardar gives the impression of someone who has seen enough of the world to be skeptical of it, but not so much as to have stopped caring. The Sea of Governance is a place where power takes on formal shape, and it remains to be seen how Vardar will reckon with that. For now, they stand at the threshold with the rest, having come a long way to get here.
