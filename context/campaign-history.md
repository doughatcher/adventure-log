# Campaign Background

This file provides campaign context for the AI. Edit it to add world-building, backstory, and setting notes that should persist across sessions.

## Setting
The campaign takes place across a world of sea trade, ancient ruins, and political intrigue. The party operates in and around the Sea of Governance and its surrounding ports and shards.

## Party
Five adventurers who have come together through circumstance — their backgrounds, motivations, and relationships are emerging through play.

## Campaign Tone
Gritty but heroic. Combat is dangerous. NPCs have agency. The world moves whether or not the party is watching.
